About ResQMap

ResQMap is an India-centric disaster management platform designed to predict, visualize, and respond to disasters in real time. It combines AI-powered risk prediction, community-driven reporting, and rescuer coordination into one unified system.

The Problem

Natural disasters like floods, earthquakes, and cyclones affect millions each year. Current systems are often slow, disconnected, and lack real-time community participation. ResQMap addresses these challenges by integrating live data and collaborative reporting.

The Solution

Using satellite feeds, weather APIs, and AI models, ResQMap predicts disaster-prone zones. A 3D interactive globe visualizes affected areas with heatmaps and live reports. Citizens can raise alerts instantly, while rescuers use the system to get shortest paths, live updates, and on-site team counts.

Our Vision

ResQMap envisions a future where technology and community come together to save lives. By empowering both citizens and responders, we aim to create a faster, smarter, and more resilient disaster management ecosystem.

Disaster Prediction

ML models forecast disaster risks in real time through historical data, giving communities critical time to prepare.

Risk Visualization

A 3D interactive globe highlights affected areas with detailed heatmaps and satellite insights.

Community Reporting and Rescuer Login

Citizens can report disasters in their locality instantly, creating a collaborative safety network and the Rescuers can login to get an extensive interface for more elaborate information about the disaster site.

How ResQMap Works

Reliable APIs

ResQMap integrates real-time weather, seismic, and satellite APIs from authentic agencies. These data streams feed into ML models that predict potential disasters before they happen.

Crowd Reporting

Citizens can report incidents instantly. These reports appear live on the 3D globe with markers, creating a real-time disaster map powered by the community.

Rescuer Dashboard

Verified rescuers log in to access a powerful dashboard. It shows the nearest disaster hotspot, calculates the shortest safe path to reach there, and displays how many rescuers are already on site.
